## Email Best Practices

### Subject Line Guidelines
- Keep under 50 characters (mobile)
- Use numbers when possible ("3 ways to...", "67% win rate")
- Create curiosity without clickbait
- Personalize when relevant ([First Name])
- A/B test everything

### Preheader Text Guidelines
- 40-100 characters
- Complement subject line (don't repeat)
- Include CTA or benefit
- Never leave blank (defaults to ugly code)

### Body Copy Guidelines
- Start with benefit, not feature
- Use short paragraphs (2-3 lines max)
- Include white space (scannable)
- One primary CTA per email
- Mobile-first formatting

### CTA Button Guidelines
- Action-oriented text ("Claim Discount" not "Click Here")
- High contrast colors
- Minimum 44x44px (mobile tap target)
- Repeat CTA if email is long
- Track clicks for optimization

## A/B Testing Ideas

### Subject Lines
- Question vs statement
- Emoji vs no emoji
- Personalization vs generic
- Benefit vs curiosity

### Send Times
- Morning (8-10 AM) vs afternoon (1-3 PM) vs evening (7-9 PM)
- Weekday vs weekend
- Immediate vs delayed

### Content
- Short vs long copy
- Text-heavy vs image-heavy
- Single CTA vs multiple CTAs
- Formal vs casual tone

### CTAs
- Button color (blue vs green vs orange)
- Button text ("Start Free Trial" vs "Get Started" vs "Try It Free")
- Button placement (top vs middle vs bottom)

### Required Elements
- Physical mailing address (footer)
- Unsubscribe link (one-click)
- Company name and contact info
- "You're receiving this because..." explanation

### GDPR Compliance
- Explicit consent for EU users
- Easy data export/deletion
- Clear privacy policy link
- Opt-in confirmation (double opt-in)

### CAN-SPAM Compliance
- Accurate "From" name and email
- Honest subject lines (no deception)
- Clear identification as advertisement (if applicable)
- Honor unsubscribe requests within 10 days
